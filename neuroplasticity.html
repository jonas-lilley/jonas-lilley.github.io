Neuroplasticity

How does it benefit people?

The ability to create new neural conecctions can bring a plethora of benefits such as:

  • Improved Learning and Skill Development
  • Recovery from Injury or Illness
  • Mental Health Improvement
  • Breaking Bad Habits and Forming Good Ones

    • How will i engage with it?

  • Exercising regularly

    • e.g I try to run 4x a week and I play basketball socially

  • Challenging myself

    • e.g having convosation I don't want to have and Challenging the bad habits I have formed

  • Being mindful of my Thoughts

    • e.g being more aware of negative spirals and recognsing that I am not the thoughts, then giving myself advice like I am talking to a friend

    What are some ways to increase it?

  • Stay Socially Engaged
  • Use Visualization and Mental Rehearsal
  • Practice Regular Learning

    Mindset

    What and why is it relevant?

    A growth mindset is the belief that abilities, intelligence, and talents can be developed over time through effort, learning, and persistence. These are some of the benefits:

  • See challenges as opportunities to grow
  • Embrace mistakes as part of the learning process
  • Persist in the face of setbacks
  • Believe effort leads to mastery

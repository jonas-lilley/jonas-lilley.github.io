Emotional intelligence
What is emotional intelligence?
Emotional intelligence refers to ones abilty to percieve, control and express emotions
What is the difference between EQ and IQ?
Both are terms that relate to problem solving.
EQ relates to solving emotional problems and IQ relates to solving cognitive problems
An example of an emotional problem would be successfully having a conversation with a friend on a topic you disagree about.
An example of a cognitive problem would be how fast you recognise patterns.
Why is EQ important?
Haivng good EQ statistically means that you're more likely to be happier and more successful.
